## The distilled spine (for quick reference)
- **The problem (two-part):** (1) the **stigma to seek care**; (2) once you do, **you can't explain it well — the way
  you say it makes you hard to diagnose.** People end up self-searching for an answer when what they needed was help
  *telling it how they feel.*
- **The mechanism:** an AI that **extracts objective + subjective truths, pieces them together, and reflects back**
  to dig at the root — *as easy as keeping a log of your condition.*
- **The output:** you **connect the dots and draft your own report** (recommendations + the *why*) — **not a
  diagnosis, but a story a provider can understand.**
- **The flow:** **Grok Voice** intro (just introducing yourself / getting to know what's going on) → conversational
  **onboarding questions** (how you're feeling — use the best industry-validated questions) → an **iMessage agent**
  that keeps track and stays with you → connect dots → draft the report.
- **Addressable for:** anxiety · depression · pains & sickness · chronic illness.
- **The build:** super-simple interface + interaction, a **really sexy front end**; the stack supports + builds out
  as much as it can while story/mission firm up; produce **HTML diagrams** of the backend process flow + how it
  applies in the **modular system**. A **two-eyes watcher** rides along the build (anti-overcomplication, refactor
  for optimal code, tests that aren't tunnel-visioned + are verifiable live).

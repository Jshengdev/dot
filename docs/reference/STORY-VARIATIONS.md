# STORY-VARIATIONS — five lenses on the DOT story (pick the spine)

> Same locked content, five narrative frameworks. Each foregrounds the PROBLEM + the WORLD differently. Pick the
> one that locks the vision; we graft the best of the others in. Frameworks from the storytelling skill. Then we
> narrow to the shortened max-feeling version (`STORY-BEATS.md` is the full progression).

## A — The Vulnerable Truth (vulnerable-story) · max intimacy
**Spine:** it opens with *your* wound, and the room can't look away.
> "A therapist once told me there was nothing wrong with me. He was right — based on what I told him. Because I'd
> spent my whole life making it smaller than it is."
- **Problem foregrounded:** you can't be understood, because surviving taught you to hide.
- **The world it paints:** one where you don't have to perform your pain to be believed.
- **Feels like:** raw, undeniable, personal. **Risk:** demands you be that exposed on stage.

## B — The Earned Insight (insight-narrative) · max "aha"
**Spine:** you went looking for one thing and found the truth nobody designs for.
> "I cold-emailed strangers to find their pain and the cure they wanted. That's not what I found. They didn't want
> the cure — they wanted to feel normal. And everyone building for them is solving the wrong problem."
- **Problem foregrounded:** it's not access — it's that the people who need care most can't make themselves understood.
- **The world it paints:** one where your *story*, not a form, is the input to care.
- **Feels like:** smart, category-reframing. **Risk:** intellectual unless anchored to a human moment.

## C — The Two Worlds (vision-narrative) · max scale
**Spine:** paint the dangerous present against the future you're building.
> "Tonight, 700 million people will pour their pain into an AI that just agrees with them — some of them toward
> harm. That's the world without us. The world with us: you talk, you're understood, and you walk into your doctor
> already seen."
- **Problem foregrounded:** the dangerous present — alone with a sycophantic AI.
- **The world it paints:** the AND world — AI *and* providers, the future of care.
- **Feels like:** big, aspirational, hopeful. **Risk:** grand/abstract without a personal anchor.

## D — Feel the Invisibility (empathy-story) · max immersion
**Spine:** put the room *inside* the experience before you explain anything.
> "Imagine knowing something is wrong — and every time you try to say it, it comes out smaller than it is. Until
> even you start to believe you're fine. That's where most people live. Unseen, by their doctors and by themselves."
- **Problem foregrounded:** the *felt* experience of not being believed.
- **The world it paints:** one where something finally reflects the truth back.
- **Feels like:** immersive, you feel it in your body. **Risk:** lives or dies on precise, vivid language.

## E — We Can't Stay Here (change-story) · max urgency
**Spine:** the status quo is collapsing, and the shortcut is dangerous — there's a third way.
> "The safety net is being cut — hotlines shut down, mental-health staff gone — while 700 million people turn to an
> AI that people-pleases them toward harm. We don't have to choose between a broken system and a dangerous shortcut."
- **Problem foregrounded:** the broken system + the dangerous shortcut, at scale, now.
- **The world it paints:** the bridge that keeps everyone safe.
- **Feels like:** urgent, mobilizing. **Risk:** fear-led if not balanced with hope.

## My read (for max FEELING + the problem + the world)
The most *feeling-forward* spine is **D (Feel the Invisibility)** or **A (Vulnerable Truth)** — they make the room
*feel* the problem before a single fact. The most *winnable hybrid*: **open on A/D (feel it) → turn on B (the earned
insight) → land the world with C (the two worlds) → DOT.** But you only need to lock ONE spine; the rest graft in as
beats. If I had to pick one that locks the vision and carries max impact: **D, with your A line as the gut-punch
inside it.**

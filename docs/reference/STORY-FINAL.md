> it leads you astray, because AI is built to agree with you.
>
> So we're building for a future where **AI and people help each other feel normal again** — where we fight the
> stigma of personal care by giving you back the agency to **own and carry your own health data**, in a way that
> finally helps both you and the providers who want to be there for you.
>

> check-ins** over time — say you feel something every three hours, and it's there three hours later — building the
> holistic, objective truth of what's really happening.
>
> So you can finally be **heard.** And your provider can finally **help.**

- **Staggered check-ins** = the longitudinal mechanism. Stories are exaggerated in the moment; DOT samples over time
  ("you said every 3 hours — checking in") to build the holistic objective truth. (New feature to reflect in

- **Patient agency = own + transport your own health data** — the event's literal theme language; benefits both, esp. providers.
